Meet the Beach Ultimate Commission

The Beach Ultimate Commission role is:

  • Chair Beach Ultimate committee discussions
  • Maintain WFDF version of the rules of Beach Ultimate
  • Develop and implement eligibility rules
  • Develop and implement sanctioned event formats, schedules and seeding
  • The Beach Ultimate Committee shall address such issues as may affect participants in Beach Ultimate and oversee the world championship competitions, which shall be held in accordance with policies established by the Congress. (by-laws)
  • Bids for such world championships shall be solicited and approved by the Beach Committee or a subcommittee designated for such purpose, subject to the approval of the Congress. (by-laws)
  • All decisions of the Beach Ultimate Committee may be appealed to the Board, and the rulings of the Board on such matters shall be subject to approval by the Congress (by-laws).
  • Board Member Tasks

If you would like to join these initiatives, or suggest others, please do get in touch.
